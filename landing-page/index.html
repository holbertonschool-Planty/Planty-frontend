About Our App

Discover the ultimate plant care app! Our mobile application immerses you in the fascinating world of gardening, offering a comprehensive guide to plant care. Whether you're an enthusiastic beginner or an experienced gardener, our user-friendly platform provides detailed information on a wide range of plant species. Receive reminders for watering, learn about ideal lighting and environmental conditions for each plant, and find solutions for common problems.

Notification System

Users have the flexibility to configure personalized notifications aligned with specific reminders they choose. These reminders can encompass various aspects such as watering schedules or warnings about extreme environmental conditions.

Real-time Data from Arduino Sensors

The app seamlessly connects with pots equipped with Arduino sensors. These sensors can measure parameters like soil moisture, light intensity, and temperature. Based on the collected data, the app can send automatic alerts if it detects abnormal conditions or if special attention is required. For example, if soil moisture falls below a critical threshold, the app could notify the user to water the plant.

Intuitive User Experience

The app offers a seamless and visually appealing experience with intuitive navigation, engaging design, and interactive features, ensuring users can effortlessly monitor and care for their plants across different devices.

How The App Works?

Sign-Up

Begin your plant care journey by signing up for the app. Provide basic information to create your account and gain access to a personalized plant care dashboard.

Link to Your Arduino-Equipped Pot (Optional)

Connect your plant's environment to the app by linking it to an Arduino-equipped pot. Follow easy setup instructions for seamless integration, enabling real-time data transmission and comprehensive monitoring.

Add Your Plant

Personalize your plant profile by adding details such as species and set preferences for notifications based on your plant's needs. Tailor the app to suit your unique plant care requirements.

Take Control of Your Plant

Empower yourself with real-time insights. Monitor humidity, temperature, and sunlight exposure. Receive personalized notifications for specific care needs. Take control of your plant's health with informed decisions, all at your fingertips.
